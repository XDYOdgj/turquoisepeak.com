Leanaco Yard Drain Emitter

Efficiently manage excess water in your yard with the Leanaco Yard Drain Emitter. Designed to handle heavy rain and irrigation, this innovative solution ensures water flows smoothly and quickly away from your property, preventing waterlogging and backflow.

With its unique inclined angle, the emitter is engineered to avoid clogging from leaves, dirt, and small animals, making it an ideal fit for sump pump outlets, detention basins, and storm drain systems. The sleek design not only follows the natural slope of your yard but also enhances safety by eliminating tripping hazards.

Constructed from high-quality, UV-resistant materials, the Leanaco Yard Drain Emitter is built to withstand harsh weather conditions and heavy use. Its easy installation makes it simple to integrate into your existing drainage setup, ensuring long-term performance and reliability.

Key Features

  • Efficient water drainage during heavy rain or irrigation
  • Inclined angle design for rapid flow and debris prevention
  • Compatible with 4-inch PVC SCH 40 pipe and various systems
  • Blends seamlessly with landscape for aesthetic appeal
  • Durable UV-resistant materials for long-lasting use

Why Choose It?

This emitter is a must-have for homeowners looking to keep their yards dry and well-maintained. Its low-maintenance requirement ensures that you can enjoy a clean, safe outdoor space without the hassle of constant upkeep. Whether you're facing seasonal flooding or need a reliable drainage solution, the Leanaco Yard Drain Emitter offers the performance and durability you expect.
