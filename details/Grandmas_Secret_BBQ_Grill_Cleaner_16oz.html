Grandma's Secret BBQ Grill Cleaner 16oz

Keep your outdoor grilling equipment in prime condition with Grandma's Secret BBQ Grill Cleaner. This reliable 16oz cleaner is designed to tackle tough grime, burnt-on grease, and carbon buildup effortlessly, without harsh chemicals or excessive scrubbing. Whether you're cleaning grates, rotisserie spits, drip pans, or any other grilling surface, it ensures a spotless finish every time. The formula is gentle enough to be used around food, making it a trusted choice for all your BBQ needs, indoors or outdoors.

Why Choose Grandma's Secret BBQ Grill Cleaner?

  • Versatile cleaning for BBQ grates, tools, rotisserie, drip pans, and more
  • Powerful grease removal with no harsh chemicals
  • Leaves no streaks or oily residue for a spotless finish
  • Effortless cleanup with a deep penetrating formula
  • Made in the USA for safe and effective kitchen cleaning

How to Use

Spray Grandma's Secret BBQ Grill Cleaner on cool surfaces of your grill. Let it sit for about a minute to break down residue, then scrub and wipe for a clean, residue-free shine. This all-purpose cleaner is perfect for removing sticky marinades, charred meats, and melted cheese, helping you maintain your grilling tools with ease.

Perfect for Every Grilling Adventure

Whether you're prepping for a backyard barbecue or a weekend gathering, Grandma's Secret BBQ Grill Cleaner makes cleanup quick, easy, and mess-free. Its zero-streak formula ensures your grilling setup looks like new, so you can focus on the joy of cooking and the delicious flavors without worrying about the hassle of cleaning.
